## About the Tournament

Join us for our legendary **Monthly Spades Tournament** - where skill meets sophistication. This isn't your typical card game; it's an experience.

Every last Saturday of the month, we transform The Velvet Lounge into a competitive arena where the best spades players in the city come to prove their worth.

### What to Expect

- **Professional Setup**: Tournament-style seating with premium card tables
- **Live Scoreboard**: Real-time bracket updates on large screens
- **VIP Atmosphere**: Full bar service, gourmet appetizers, and lounge seating
- **Entertainment**: DJ spinning classic R&B, soul, and contemporary hits
- **Community**: Network with like-minded professionals in their 30s, 40s, and beyond

### Tournament Format

- **Teams of 2** (you can register solo and we'll match you)
- **Single Elimination Bracket**
- **Best of 3 games** for finals
- **Books to 7** (traditional rules)
- **No Boston allowed** in championship rounds

### Why This Event is Special

Our Spades Tournament has become the premier social event for adults who appreciate competition, camaraderie, and class. It's where friendships are forged, rivalries are born, and legends are made.

Whether you're a seasoned player or just enjoy a good game, this event brings together the community in a way that only spades can.

### Registration Details

Space is limited to **16 teams (32 players)**. Early registration recommended as this event sells out every month.

**What's Included:**
- Tournament entry for your team
- Complimentary welcome drinks
- Appetizer buffet
- Event t-shirt
- Access to after-party lounge

Bring your A-game. Bring your partner. Let's play.
