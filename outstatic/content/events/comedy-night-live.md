## A Night of Laughter

Sometimes you just need to laugh. Join us for **Comedy Night Live** - an evening of top-tier stand-up comedy featuring nationally touring comedians who know how to make grown folks laugh.

### The Lineup

**Headliner: Marcus "The Kingpin" Johnson**
With appearances on HBO, Showtime, and Comedy Central, Marcus brings sharp observations about relationships, aging, and navigating life after 30. His style is intelligent, relatable, and hilarious.

**Feature: Keisha Williams**
A rising star in the comedy world, Keisha's takes on modern dating, career struggles, and family dynamics will have you crying from laughter. As seen on Netflix's "Comedy Lineup."

**Opening Act: DJ "Smooth" Robinson**
Local favorite DJ brings crowd work and improvisation to another level. His energy sets the perfect tone for the evening.
