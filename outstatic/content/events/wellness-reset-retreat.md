## A Weekend to Reset and Renew

Escape the daily grind and join us for a transformative **Wellness Reset Retreat** designed specifically for adults seeking to recenter, recharge, and reconnect with what matters most.

### The Philosophy

Health at 30, 40, 50+ requires intentionality. This retreat isn't about quick fixes - it's about sustainable practices, mindful living, and community support for the journey ahead.

### Your Weekend Experience

**Friday Evening:**
- Welcome reception with herbal tea ceremony
- Group intention-setting circle
- Restorative yoga session
- Farm-to-table dinner under the stars

**Saturday:**
- Sunrise meditation & breathwork
- Nutritious breakfast buffet
- Choice of morning workshops:
  - Strength training for mature bodies
  - Hormone health & aging gracefully
  - Stress management techniques
- Gourmet lunch
- Afternoon spa treatments (60-min massage or facial included)
- Optional nature hike or pool time
- Evening sound bath meditation
- Community dinner & fireside conversations

**Sunday:**
- Gentle yoga flow
- Brunch & nutrition workshop
- Integration circle: Taking wellness home
- Closing ceremony

### Wellness Workshops

**Nutrition for Longevity**
Understanding how nutritional needs change with age and practical meal planning strategies.

**Movement Medicine**
Low-impact, high-benefit exercises for joint health, flexibility, and strength.

**Mental Wellness**
Managing stress, anxiety, and the psychological aspects of aging gracefully.

**Sleep Optimization**
Science-backed strategies for better rest and recovery.

**Hormonal Balance**
Natural approaches to hormonal changes and holistic health solutions.
