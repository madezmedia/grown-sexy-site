## An Evening of Refinement

Indulge in an exquisite evening where fine wines meet fascinating conversations. Our **Wine & Wisdom** series is designed for the sophisticated palate and the curious mind.

### The Experience

This isn't just a wine tasting - it's a journey through terroir, tradition, and taste. Led by certified sommeliers, you'll explore carefully curated selections while engaging in meaningful dialogue with fellow connoisseurs.

### What You'll Taste

**Five Premium Wines:**
- **Sparkling**: Champagne from Reims, France
- **White**: Chardonnay from Sonoma Coast, California
- **Rosé**: Provence blend from Southern France
- **Red**: Malbec from Mendoza, Argentina
- **Dessert**: Port from Douro Valley, Portugal

Each pour is paired with artisan cheeses, cured meats, and gourmet accompaniments selected to enhance the tasting experience.

### The Setting

Bordeaux Wine Bar & Gallery offers an intimate, gallery-style space with contemporary art, soft lighting, and comfortable seating. The ambiance is designed for relaxation and connection.

### What Makes This Special

- **Small Group Format**: Limited to 40 guests for intimate discussions
- **Educational**: Learn about wine regions, production methods, and pairing principles
- **Social**: Meet like-minded individuals who appreciate the finer things
- **Curated**: Every element is thoughtfully selected by our wine experts
- **Exclusive**: Members receive 20% off featured wines purchased during the event

### Topics of Discussion

Our facilitated conversations touch on:
- Travel experiences and wine regions visited
- Food and flavor pairing philosophies
- The art of mindful consumption
- Building a personal wine collection

### What's Included

- Five premium wine tastings (3 oz pours)
- Artisan cheese and charcuterie board
- Educational tasting notes and pairing guide
- Access to exclusive wine purchase discounts
- Light background music and ambiance

Come for the wine. Stay for the wisdom. Leave with new friends and expanded horizons.

**Dress Code:** Smart Casual
